Mango Lassi

The Mango Lassi is a traditional Indian drink, originally from Punjab, a region in northwestern India. The drink, which has calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

Ingredients

  • Fresh good quality Mango pulp
  • Fresh curd
  • Sugar
  • Ice

Preparation

  1. Put mango pulp, curd, sugar, and ice cubes in a blender and mix them well for 2-3 mins
  2. Pour it and garnish it with fresh mint leaves and enjoy
