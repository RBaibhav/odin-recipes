Mutton Curry

This recipe is from northen part of India mainly Bihar, Jharkhand made with freshly butchered goat.

Ingredients

  • 1000gm Mutton(curry cut)
  • 3tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 3-4 Green chillies
  • 400gm onions
  • Mustard Oil
  • Cumin
  • Dry Spices
  • Asafoetida
  • meat masala
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Heat up thick bottom pot, Then add mustard oil then it heats up add cumin seeds and ginger garlic paste, let it cook for 30 sec
  2. Add yr diced onions then cook till it's golden
  3. When onions are golden add your mutton pieces and roast it for 10 mins(don't let it burn keep stirring every 2min) meat should have released it's fat and moisture
  4. Add salt and dry spices (turmeric 1 tbsp, red chilli 1.5 tbsp, coriander 2 tbsp power) and roast it for 5-10min if it's start burning add dash of water
  5. When oil is separated add water accordingly and let it simmer till meat is 90% cooked
  6. Then add 2tbsp of meat masala and simmer it for more 10 mins add water accordingly(it should have heavy milk consistency)
  7. Turn of heat from mutton pot and heat up a fresh pan and put some ghee in it
  8. Put 2 pinch of Asafoetida in the hot ghee and then put the ghee into mutton pot and cover it.
  9. Let it rest for 10min and enjoy
